REVEREND VICTOR ANAELE: A VISIONARY LEADER IN MINISTRY, MISSIONS, AND DIGITAL INNOVATION.

Reverend Victor Ifeanyi Anaele is a dynamic clergy, digital marketing expert, and visionary leader shaping lives through ministry, community outreach, and entrepreneurial innovation. Born on April 24 at New Era Hospital, Aba, Abia State, Reverend Anaele hails from Ndimara Amaegbu, Nkwerre LGA, Imo State. As the fifth child in a family of eight, he grew up in a devout Christian environment that nurtured his faith and commitment to God.

EARLY LIFE AND EDUCATION

At 16, Reverend Anaele dedicated his life to Christ, embarking on a journey of faith and service. He holds a Bachelor of Science in Agronomy from Cross River University of Technology (now UNICROSS), where he graduated with a Second Class Upper Division.

MINISTRY CAREER

Reverend Anaele’s ministerial journey began during his university days, where he pioneered the Nigeria Fellowship of Evangelical Students (NIFES) Faculty of Agriculture group and later served as the Zonal President of NIFES Calabar Zone from 2013 to 2014. His early roles included Youth Pastor at Christian Pentecostal Mission, Ehere, State Youth Secretary and Program Director in Abia State.

In 2017, he joined the Remnant Christian Network (RCN) and pastored its Aba branch, later founding Apostolic Centers in Owerri, Port Harcourt, Bayelsa, Umuahia, and Uyo. Relocating to Uyo in 2023, Reverend Anaele now serves as the resident pastor. He was ordained as a pastor in 2019 and inducted as a reverend on May 26, 2023.

LEADERSHIP AND EVANGELISM

Reverend Anaele’s passion for discipleship and missions is evident through his outreach programs targeting schools, universities, and rural communities. He is the convener of two major annual gatherings: • Eastern Watchmen Convergence: Uniting believers in Eastern Nigeria. • The Prophetic Convergence: Empowering believers in Southern Nigeria. He also oversees the Remnant Foundation School, a training institution dedicated to equipping believers and aspiring ministers with Biblical knowledge and practical skills.

INNOVATION AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP

Beyond ministry, Reverend Anaele is the Executive Director of Engraced Digital Hub, where he leverages his expertise in digital marketing to empower businesses and individuals. Certified by Facebook Nigeria and Google Africa, he also serves as the Governor of the Aspiring Entrepreneurs Program (Cohort 80) with Facebook Nigeria.

PERSONAL LIFE

Reverend Victor Anaele is married to Mrs. Comfort Victor Anaele, and their marriage is blessed with a son, Zinachimdindu Jason Victor-Anaele.

Recognitions and Achievements • Recipient of grants from Facebook Nigeria for entrepreneurial excellence. • Recognized for leadership in ministry and business innovation. • Celebrated for his mentorship and practical leadership approach.

A LEGACY OF IMPACT

Reverend Victor Ifeanyi Anaele’s life is a testament to his commitment to God, his passion for discipleship, and his drive for innovation. Through his ministry, outreach, and entrepreneurial ventures, he continues to inspire and empower a generation of believers and leaders to impact the world for Christ.
