Outreaches

As one of our core mandate is outreaches, we have an active mission team with the burden to carry the light and fire of the Gospel of God to lost souls in different areas of the state.

Activities includes;

  • Street evangelism
  • Prison outreach
  • Secondary school outreach

To be part of this, kindly reach out to the head of missions via 0907 929 8375

Project Cast Your Net

This is a bigger vision of the ministry to reach out to lost souls in rural areas and post secondary school institutions. It will also include such missions as Jesus to the bad boys were we will reach out to cultists and Jesus to the prostitutes where we reach out to prostitutes. We're currently seeking partners for this particular vision.

You can reach out to us if you're believing God that we bring the Gospel of Jesus to your area, be it a rural area or a postsecondary institution. Kindly reach out to us on our official email or contact 0803 815 7510.

Foundation School

This is a training school where everyone that intends to serve the Lord beautifully as a member of the workforce undergoes the requisite training necessary. It's an intensive school that is divided into BASIC INTERMEDIATE and ADVANCED. It holds every Saturday by 12pm when any cohort is currently running. You can reach out to the head of the foundation school via 0708 832 1407.
