About RCN Uyo

Vision

To equip ministers of the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ in Africa and Europe to become effective in the work of the ministry.

Mission

To strive for the rebirth of Apostolic Christianity in our generation. Preservation of biblical doctrine and living out the truth of God’s word as it is in the Holy Scriptures.

Vision Statement

In the conceptualization of RCN core values, we define what makes our ministry different from what others are doing. They are as follows:

  • Intercession
  • Discipleship
  • Outreach

The Book of Acts is our textbook!

In the book of Acts, we see that revival on the day of Pentecost began from the throne room to the upper room by intercession (tarry in Jerusalem), and went from the upper room to the classroom by discipleship and room to room (other parts of the world) by outreaches. Our entry into any territory is intercession, but that's not only the handle that is needed for true.

Apostolic Blueprint Ministry

Because of interaction with scriptures, we have concluded that Revival which has only the throne room and upper room (Intercession) features never lasts. Our major and first culture is intercession! But there is more! The Apostolic Church is a church of Intercessors, but they did not just pray, they also engaged in a lot of discipleship and outreaches. Acts 19 is a graphic picture of the Discipleship Approach used by Paul in Corinth. It says,

"And it came to pass, that, while Apollos was at Corinth, Paul having passed through the upper coasts came to Ephesus: and finding certain disciples, He said unto them, Have ye received the Holy Ghost since ye believed? And they said unto him, We have not so much as heard whether there be any Holy Ghost.

And he said unto them, Unto what then were ye baptized? And they said, Unto John’s baptism. Then said Paul, John verily baptized with the baptism of repentance, saying unto the people, that they should believe on him which should come after him, that is, on Christ Jesus.

When they heard this, they were baptized in the name of the Lord Jesus. And when Paul had laid his hands upon them, the Holy Ghost came on them; and they spake with tongues, and prophesied. And all the men were about twelve.

And he went into the synagogue, and spake boldly for the space of three months, disputing and persuading the things concerning the kingdom of God. But when divers were hardened and believed not, but spake evil of that way before the multitude, he departed from them, and separated the disciples, disputing daily in the school of one Tyrannus.

And this continued by the space of two years; so that all they which dwelt in Asia heard the word of the Lord Jesus, both Jews and Greeks. And God wrought special miracles by the hands of Paul: So that from his body were brought unto the sick handkerchiefs or aprons, and the diseases departed from them, and the evil spirits went out of them."

We follow the principle of scripture to implement that which we have conceptualized:

  • Fruitfulness: implementation of the Intercessory and discipleship strategy in your territory.
  • Multiplication: Raising disciples that can replicate these in different territories.
  • Dominate: Dominating the territory with the model of IDO (Intercession, Discipleship and Outreaches).

Outreaches

As one of our core mandate is outreaches, we have an active mission team with the burden to carry the light and fire of the Gospel of God to lost souls in different areas of the state.

Activities includes;

  • Street evangelism
  • Prison outreach
  • Secondary school outreach
