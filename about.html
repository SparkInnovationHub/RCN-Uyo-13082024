About RCN Uyo

Vision

To equip ministers of the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ in Africa and Europe to become effective in the work of the ministry.

Mission

To strive for the rebirth of Apostolic Christianity in our generation. Preservation of biblical doctrine and living out the truth of God’s word as it is in the Holy Scriptures.

To Set Men On Fire For Jesus!

Remnant Christian Network (RCN) is a non-denominational ministry with apostolic centers across Africa, Europe, and North America. We're dedicated to:

  • Raising intercessors, prophets, evangelists, and teachers
  • Equipping believers to access and navigate the immortal realm
  • Providing training and guidance for spiritual growth and deeper revelations of God
  • Preparing the way for the second coming of our Lord Jesus Christ

Are you fed up with playing church? Are you tired of church overridden by mammon and Babylon? Are you tired of sipping milk when you should be chewing strong meat? Are you looking to grow spiritual muscles in Christ? Are you searching for a deeper revelation of God? Do you want to be able to sift between the wheat and the chaff and gain a proper handle on the truth? Do you want to be a student of the word of God? Do you want to stand out as light in these dark days?

Welcome to Remnant Christian Network Uyo where you will be taught to access and navigate the immortal realm, receive the requisite training and equipment to stand as a soldier in the army of God.

You can call on the phone, send emails or reach us on any of our social media channels to get more information. Also, we will be glad to see you at any of our meetings.

You are welcome!
